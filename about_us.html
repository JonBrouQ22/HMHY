About Us

We are currently just me. My name is Jonathan Brou and I am trying to fill a need that I see. As someone who is
interested in volunteering, I've noticed that it's not that easy. Even using a volunteering matching service,
getting through the application process was a nightmare and each organization had their own forms. I thought
there had to be a better way. So here at Help Me Help You, we aim to serve the volunteer in as many ways as possible.
From a live map of events, to a shared organization page where news and announcements can be found in one place.
We aim to provide the user the ability to volunteer on a whim, collaborate with friends, and keep in touch
with the organizations they care about.
